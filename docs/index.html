Trump Tweets

A short story




What Wikipedia has to say...

"Trump has frequently used Twitter and other social media to comment on politicians and celebrities, and he relied on Twitter significantly to communicate during the 2016 United States presidential election."

However...

"Many of the assertions made by Trump on his Twitter account have been proven to be false."

Let's have a look, shall we?

We have obtained over 32 thousand verified Trump Tweets from the Trump Twitter Archive. We hope to help you discover some fun things about his social media prescence. Let's get started!




Our tweet word cloud.

This is a word cloud of the most used words over every single Donald Trump tweet. Pronouns and some articles have been removed (a, the, ...) since they are the most used words in the English language. It is randomly generated from the 250 most used words and will be different each time you load the page! Words used more often will be larger. Can you spot "crooked" and "hillary"? Maybe you can even spot "hope", but be careful, it's very small...




Our interactive graphs

Welcome to our interactive graphs. Here you you view the entire history of Trump tweets interactively! You can filter tweets by date interval using the histogram under the line chart. You can also filter by device or even day of the week using the bar chart, or by source using the pie chart. The top 10 most retweeted tweets using your filters will appear in the table below!

Here are some dates you might be interested in concerning Trump's presidential campaign:

You can reset your filters at anytime using the individual reset buttons per chart, or using the "Reset all" button to reset all filters.
